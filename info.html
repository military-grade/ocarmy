WHATS DIFFERENT?

Unlike other websites, we don't make you pay for customization! We are made by artists, and funded by artists. Customization is always a need for our characters, and we had found it so aggravating how we can't even make our characters feel like themselves due to paywalls!

Funding us is completely optional, but it really helps us make improvements to our site.

Learn how to support us here!
MODERATION

We find moderation key, and we want to keep our site as safe as possible. We have various moderators, a key ticket and feedback system, and no unfair practices. We want to trust our community, and this includes keeping online threats away from OC ARMY!

Our moderators keep a close eye on the website. You can reach out to us via a ticket, or at ocarmy@gmail.com.

CHARACTERS AND ORIGINALITY

We love all characters of any kind! Part of this is understanding kinsonas, cbcs, and other inspired OCs. We don't think any characters are uncreative, and we fully support any and all inspired characters—as long as the original creators of the inspiration are okay with it, of course!

Character originality is also important, so we will make sure any potential copies or reuploads of your original characters are taken care of properly in a timely manner.

We are a small and growing community, and you joining us helps a lot!
